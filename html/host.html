Frequently Asked Questions

How will our family benefit by hosting a student?

Families hosting high school students should:

  • Be able to provide for an additional member of the family including a separate bed, suitable study area and three meals per day.
  • Offer a supportive environment as the student goes through her adjustment process.

  • One member of your household must have a job in tech and be willing to share their experiences
  • Familiarize your student with your city and promote participation in school and community events.
  • Provide a safe and secure environment for the student to live and learn.

How will our family benefit by hosting a student?

Hosting a female student is a rewarding experience for your whole family. You’ll start a life-long relationship with your new "daughter. Members of your family will gain a broader perspective, learning more about the day-to-day life of somone from a different background.

How long do we have to host our student?

Four months.

How will our exchange student get around?

The host family is expected to provide transportation. Though students will take the school bus when available, hosts must make sure the student has transportation to school each day.

What if the student needs medical attention?

Your student is provided with health insurance that will cover any expenses should they require a visit to the doctor. Before a student is accepted into most programs, they must undergo a complete physical exam and submit a certified report documenting that they are in good health.
